The Electricity Revolution

Hello, my name is André Leone and I am a graduate student in electrical engineering at the Federal University of Paraná. Located in the south of Brazil. I would like to talk to you about electricity replacing the oil as a primary source of energy. I like to call it the electricity revolution.

To illustrate the electricity revolution, I would like to highlight two points:
  • The increase in electricity consumption
  • How it impacts ours cities.

The increase in electricity consumption:

it's simple !!!!, it increases because electrical equipment are increasing, how example we have the electrical devices in our homes and also the new cars using electricity, the electric vehicles.

How it Impacts ours cities:

If we consider replacing oil by electricity, the first change will be in the environment, with cleaner air and less pollutants. And the second change will be in the landscape of your cities through generation of electric energy using the sun, the wind and the waters.
