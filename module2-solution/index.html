The Electricity Revolution

Revolution

Oil has long been the primary source of energy, but we are about to experience a revolution. The revolution of electricity, with electricity replacing oil and becoming the primary source of energy.

Consumption

Electricity consumption is growing, because electrical equipment is increasing. As an example we have cell phones, 8 billion around the world, and electric vehicles (which will reach 200 million EVs by 2030);

Impacts

If we consider replacing oil by electricity, the first change will be in the environment, with cleaner air and less pollutants. And the second change will be in the landscape of your cities through generation of electric energy using the sun, the wind and the water.
